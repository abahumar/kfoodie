# Maggi Tiram

Kelantan usually takes instant noodles to the next level. **Maggi Tiram** features spicy curry noodles cooked in a claypot, loaded with fresh, plump oysters and sometimes prawns and squid.

## Flavour Profile
The broth is rich, spicy, and savory from the seafood. A hearty meal for late-night cravings.
