# Nasi Kerabu Golok

If you are looking for authentic Kelantanese breakfast, **Nasi Kerabu Golok** is the place to be.

## Why it's special
The blue rice is naturally colored using *Bunga Telang* (Butterfly Pea Flower). Served with spicy fish crackers, salted egg, and the signature *ayam percik*.

### Details
- **Location:** Jalan Hospital, Kota Bharu
- **Opening Hours:** 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- **Price:** RM 6.00 - RM 10.00
