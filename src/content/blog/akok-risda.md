# Akok Risda

**Akok** is a traditional kuih made from eggs, coconut milk, flour, and sugar. **Akok Risda** is famous for its rich, eggy flavor and wrinkled golden-brown crust.

## Perfect Tea Time Snack
It is best enjoyed warm with a cup of tea. The texture is soft, almost custard-like.
