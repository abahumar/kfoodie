# Colek Malah

**Colek Malah** is a unique Kelantanese fruit salad (rojak) that uses a thick, savory, and spicy sauce. It typically includes fruits like mango, pineapple, and cucumber, often with added seafood like squid.

## Where to find it
You can find Colek Malah at various night markets and roadside stalls throughout Kota Bharu.
